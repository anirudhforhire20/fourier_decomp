Welcome to Virtual Stock Exchange.


A stock exchange on the internet that's open to anyone any time.


This is not a real stock exchange which is in any way affiliated to real life publically traded organizations.


Here you can buy and trade stocks of virtual companies either from companies or from other users. Just go to marketplace and choose from a list of hundreds of existing IPOs or begin buying directly from other players.
Your account starts off with $100,000 (not real obviously) which you can use to buy stocks. Your account also keeps track of all the transactions you've made. You have to keep track of your portfolio yourself though, this site does not provides a portfolio.
Once you've decided which deal you want to make, you can either purchase it directly or request a bid. Both methods will send a proposal to the user which placed that deal--incase of IPOs you cannot bid and you'll directly buy the stocks. If the user accepts your offer, congratulations! you two made a deal!

This site works exactly like a real life stock exchange i.e public deals decide the value of the stock. Later we'll add more features like charts, graphs, and hopefully a portfolio. In the meantime, enjoy your stay and watchout for more!

protip: Use transactions table for bookeeping.
